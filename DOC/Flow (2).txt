# TravelTales Flow Chart

## 1. Overall System Flow Diagram

```text
[Creator / Consumer]
          |
          v
     [Web Browser]
          |
          v
   [React Frontend]
          |
          v
[Node.js / Express API]
      /             \
     /               \
    v                 v
[Cloud Storage]   [MongoDB Atlas / Cosmos DB]
  (Photos)        (Users, Metadata,
                    Comments, Ratings)
```

---

## 2. Photo Upload Flow Diagram

```text
[Creator Login]
       |
       v
[Open Upload Page]
       |
       v
[Select Photo + Enter Metadata]
       |
       v
[Frontend Sends Request]
       |
       v
[Backend Validates User + File]
       |
       +-------------------+
       |                   |
       v                   v
[Upload to Storage]   [Store Metadata in DB]
       |                   |
       +---------+---------+
                 |
                 v
      [Send Success Response]
                 |
                 v
      [Display Uploaded Photo]
```

---

## 3. Consumer Interaction Flow Diagram

```text
[Consumer Opens Gallery]
         |
         v
[Frontend Requests Photos]
         |
         v
[Backend Fetches Photo Data]
         |
         +-------------------+
         |                   |
         v                   v
 [Read Metadata]       [Read Image URL]
   [Database]         [Cloud Storage/CDN]
         |                   |
         +---------+---------+
                   |
                   v
      [Frontend Displays Photos]
                   |
                   v
       [Consumer Opens Photo Page]
                   |
                   v
      [Add Comment or Rating]
                   |
                   v
      [Backend Stores Interaction]
                   |
                   v
      [Updated Photo Details Shown]
```

## 4. Search Flow Diagram

```text
[Consumer Enters Search Term]
              |
              v
   [Frontend Sends Search Request]
              |
              v
   [Backend Receives Search Query]
              |
              v
[Database Searches Title / City / Country]
              |
              v
     [Matching Results Returned]
              |
              v
   [Frontend Displays Search Results]
```

---

## 5. Authentication Flow Diagram

```text
[User Opens Login Page]
          |
          v
 [Enter Email + Password]
          |
          v
[Frontend Sends Login Request]
          |
          v
 [Backend Verifies User Data]
          |
          v
 [Generate JWT Token]
          |
          v
[Return Token to Frontend]
          |
          v
 [Store Token for Session]
          |
          v
[Grant Access Based on Role]
```
